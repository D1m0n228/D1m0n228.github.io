ADVANCED X-RAY

POWERED BY X-RAY 1.6

Modified X-Ray 1.6 engine with numerous graphics and gameplay fixes, fixes of original
bugs, flexible configuration of many engine parameters, and support for RFP and CR.

  • AP-PRO

Gameplay and graphical modification, which includes not only
changes in weather cycles and lighting, but also engine and shader changes. This addon is developed primarily for other modmakers. A feature
of the modification is the ability to switch the mode from default to winter or vice versa (you will only need to add the appropriate textures,
sounds, etc. to your project), as well as a relatively large range of engine edits aimed at improving the graphic component.

THE STORY OF PUKHTINSKY

STORY MOD BASED ON THE AXR ENGINE

The story of Pukhtinsky is the third modification of Tolik Bomzh's universe, which tells about the formation of Lekha Pukhta as a bandit, and
Tolik as the future king of the landfill.
The mod significantly expands the game universe, adding new places, mechanics, items, tasks, new and already familiar characters from Tolik's
Adventures. Go the hard way from the errand six to authority, and show the whole neighborhood who Lech Pukhta is!
